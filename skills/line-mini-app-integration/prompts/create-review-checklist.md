# Prompt: create-review-checklist

Generate review-readiness and compliance checklist.

Checklist items:
- [ ] Service category allowed under LINE MINI App policy (no religious, adult, gambling, weapons, etc.)
- [ ] Privacy policy discloses third-party sharing, timing, service provider relationship
- [ ] Channel description explains actual service, not just company slogan
- [ ] Channel icon, name, description, privacy policy URL are accurate and current
- [ ] Review and Published endpoints are functionally aligned
- [ ] Test scenarios/accounts/products provided for order/reservation/payment flows
- [ ] In-app purchase approval completed first (if applicable)
- [ ] Certified provider status confirmed (Thailand/Taiwan)
- [ ] Verified-only features not assumed in Published before approval
- [ ] Re-review-sensitive settings tracked separately

Also include policy category exclusions list and re-review trigger fields.
